Bio

Wei Zhang is now a Senior Researcher in JD AI Research. He received his Ph.D degree from City University of Hong Kong, Hong Kong, China in 2015. He was a visiting scholar in DVMM group of Columbia University, New York, USA in 2014. His research interests include visual recognition and generation. He was in VIREO (superviser: Chong-Wah Ngo) during 2011 ~ 2015.

Looking for motivated intern students. Drop me an email if interested.
